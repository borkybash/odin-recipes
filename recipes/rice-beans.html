Two Can Rice and Beans

Description

Super easy rice and beans recipe that's not only tasty but gurantee to bring the heat to your dutch ovens.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz can black beans
  • 10 oz can diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
  • 1/2 cup dried jasmine or basmati rice
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add half cup dry rice to a 2-4 quart pot. Add 1.5 cups water to pot with pinch of salt. Bring to boil, then cover and bring down to simmer for 25 minutes.

  2. When rice is done, add can of drained black beans and can of diced tomatoes and mix the shit out of it.

  3. Mix in any other spices or salt as needed and enjoy.
