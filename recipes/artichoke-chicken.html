Baked Artichoke Chicken

Description

This is my go to date night meal that is a different but easy baked chicken dish that is sure to impress with it's unique combination of artichoke, tomato, and garlic that makes this a slammin' yet filling meal for two.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • fresh cracked pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Ib chicken breasts or tenders
  • 14 oz can quartered artichoke hearts
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.

  2. In bowl, combine tomatoes, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, salt and pepper and mix.

  3. Drain artichoke hearts and place half in a baking dish. Lay chicken spread out on top and season with salt and pepper. Pour the tomato mixture on top of the chicken.

  4. Bake for 30 minutes, then take out of oven and place mozzarella over top of chicken and bake in broiler for about 5 minutes or until cheese turns a golden brown.
